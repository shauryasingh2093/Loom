Screen 1 — Step Outside (Guidance Screen)

This screen opens when the user taps "Step Outside" in the Companion screen suggestions list.

Title:
Step Outside

Subtitle:
Clear environment threads

Instruction text:

A short change of environment can calm your sensory threads.
Step outside, take a few slow breaths, and notice the air and sounds around you.

Visual element:

Show environment threads slightly tangled but beginning to relax.

Duration indicator:

5 min reset

Primary button:

Start reset

Secondary option:

Skip activity

Screen 2 — Step Outside (Completion Screen)

After the activity finishes.

Title:

Nice reset

Message:

Your environment threads are calming.
Your weave is becoming more balanced.

Visual:

Threads appear smoother and less tangled.

Primary button:

See your weave

This returns the user to the main Loom dashboard screen.

Screen 3 — Body Scan (Instruction Screen)

This screen opens when the user taps "Body Scan" from the Companion suggestions list.

Title:

Body Scan

Subtitle:

Release tension in your body

Instruction text:

Slowly scan your body from head to toe.
Notice areas of tension and allow them to soften.

Show three guidance steps:

• Relax your shoulders
• Release jaw tension
• Slow your breathing

Visual element:

Threads representing body signals slowly loosening tension.

Duration indicator:

3 min scan

Primary button:

Begin body scan

Screen 4 — Body Scan (Completion Screen)

After the body scan finishes.

Title:

Body tension reduced

Message:

Your body threads have loosened and your weave is stabilizing.

Visual:

Body threads appear more relaxed and flowing smoothly.

Primary button:

Return to weave

This takes the user back to the Loom dashboard.

Flow Connection (Important)

Prototype flow should be:

Companion Screen
↓
Tap "Step Outside" → Step Outside Guidance → Step Outside Completion → Return to Weave

Companion Screen
↓
Tap "Body Scan" → Body Scan Instructions → Body Scan Completion → Return to Weave
